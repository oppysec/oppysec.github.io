Services

Our goal is to offer services for those that wish to reclaim their online privacy and empower themselves to control their own data.


Threat Model Consultation - Threat modeling is a process by which potential threats, such as structural vulnerabilities can be identified, enumerated, and prioritized  all from a hypothetical attacker’s point of view. We focus on design-level flaws that can lead to further security or privacy risk. This service is ideal for those that may be targeted by an advanced adversary.


Private Phone Number - When Susan orders food from UberEATS she is also exposing her phone number. This same number may be linked to her bank, her WhatsApp and even her doctor. This service allows you to maintain a disposable phone number you can use for certain services or to call specific people without having it linked to your real identity, keeping you and your family safe from attackers.


Cyber Investigations - We can assist those who have been the victim of cyber stalking, bullying, defamation or other illicit acts.

Web Site/Web App Security Assessment - Scan your web site for design flaws and other common web app vulnerabilities that can lead to a breach.


Oppositional Research - Oppo research is the practice of collecting information on a political opponent or other adversary that can be used to discredit or otherwise weaken them. Our team is able to create a secure and private platform where this sensitive research can be executed anonymously.


Secure Private Cloud (1 Year): Our private cloud service offers a online file storage system that we monitor and setup specifically for the clients needs. Files can be shared via mobile devices, tablets and computers. The platform we use provides granular control over user accounts and secure link sharing to share files with others.


Remote Support (2 Hour Minimum)
